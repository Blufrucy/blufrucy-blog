## About Me

Hi, I'm **贺钰堂 (Blufrucy)** — an AI Native Developer, Designer, and Creator.

I treat large language models as core components of my products, using AI workflows to take ideas from concept to real, usable software. My development approach has shifted from writing every line of code by hand to defining requirements, orchestrating Coding Agents, reviewing generated code, integrating systems, and continuously validating.

I believe the future of software development is not just about using AI to write code — it's about designing products around AI capabilities.
